What is an Anchor Event?

A real historical event tied to a specific year — plague, invention, religious movement, climate shift. It fires automatically when the game reaches that year, regardless of what players have been doing.

What is a Ripple Event?

Not tied to a year — triggered when a specific condition occurs during gameplay. These represent historical patterns: overextension, cultural diffusion, economic collapse. They should feel like consequences, not punishments.
